Education

Before coming to Northwestern, I was a postdoctoral fellow at University of Western Ontario. I’m a member of the Project NExT Brown’20 cohort. I have a certification from the Teaching Academy at JHU. I was a mentor for Canada/USA Mathcamp from 2017-2019 and an academic coordinator for the summer of 2018.

I completed my PhD from JHU in 2019 with a thesis titled An application of the h-principle to Manifold Calculus. I have an MSc in Mathematics from Chennai Mathematical Institute and a BTech in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur.
