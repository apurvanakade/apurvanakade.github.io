Order
Filed under Lockdown Diaries, May 13, 2020.

I have always loved chaos. But sadly, I find myself surrounded by too much of it. With the tables turned, I find myself trying to find order in these chaotic times. Here’s what has helped me so far:

  1. OneNote Nebo on iPad + iPencil for keeping a daily journal
  2. Joplin for organizing notes
  3. Goodnotes for math
  4. Ticktick Google Keep for making daily checklists
  5. A very convenient Pomodoro timer
  6. Zoom meetings with friends and family
  7. Duolingo to keep myself busy
  8. Work

I really hope when this crisis ends (which I know is never) I can get back to complaining about too much order and wanting more chaos and anarchy. That is such a friendly territory for me, this is just too foreign.

Sigh.
