Course Description

Students who intend to pursue a degree in Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Actuarial Sciences, Physics or Astronomy should take this course. Techniques of integration; The Mean Value Theorem and its consequences; series, Taylor series with applications; parametric and polar curves with applications; first order linear and separable differential equations with applications.
