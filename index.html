I'm a postdoc in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Western Ontario working with Dan Christensen and Chris Kapulkin.

In the Fall of 2020, I'll be teaching Math 2151A (Discrete Math for Engineers).

I work in the area of algebraic topology and homotopy theory. My broad interests are in understanding how homotopy theoretic tools can be used to solve problems from other areas of math.

While learning homotopy type theory, I came across the Lean theorem prover and have since gotten obsessed with it.

I am interested in improving math education. I want to modernize math education and bring it to the 21st century through the use of new technological innovations in teaching and in the process make it more accessible and equitable.
