Résumé

CV   Teaching Evaluations

I did my bachelor's in computer science and engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. I did a masters in mathematics from Chennai Mathematical Institute followed by Ph.D. in mathematics from Johns Hopkins University.

Since 2019, I have been a postdoctoral fellow at University of Western Ontario. Starting Fall 2021, I'll be a postdoctoral lecturer at Northwestern University. I'm a member of the Project NExT Brown'20 cohort. I also have a Certificate of Completion from the Teaching Academy at JHU.

I was a mentor for Canada/USA Mathcamp from 2017-2019 and an academic coordinator for the summer of 2018. Below is a complete list of courses I've taught so far.
